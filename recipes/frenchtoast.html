French Toast

This french toast is some the best I've ever tried and I would hate for anyone to miss out on it!

Below is a step by step proccess explaining how to make this wonderful recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg yolks
    • 4 pieces of french toast bread
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  1. Seperate the egg yolks and dump out the whites into a sink, then add the milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla into a small bowl and whisk until a smooth texture is achieved.
  2. Transfer liquid into a flat container with room for your bread and dip the bread for about 15-20 seconds each side, or until preferred texture is achieved.
  3. Heat a pan with half a tablespoon of butter and half a tablespoon of olive oil. Once the two are melted add the french toast. Allow the first side to brown and flip immediately.
  4. Sprinkle sugar on the newly browned side then flip it back down with the sugar against the pan. Repeat this proccess until both sides are carmelized.
  5. Congratulations, you did it!! Now enjoy this custard french toast.
